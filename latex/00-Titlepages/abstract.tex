\begin{abstract}
\noindent
This note reports LHCb's first measurement of the $\CP$ violation observables
$S$ and $C$ in the decay channel $\BdToDD$. The analysed data sample corresponds
to an integrated luminosity of $\SI{3}{fb^{-1}}$ of $pp$ collisions at centre-
of-mass energies of \SIlist{7;8}{\TeV}. Using an unbinned maximum likelihood fit
the tagged, time-dependent analysis reveals the $\CP$ observables to be
\begin{align*}
  \SDD &=  1.14 \pm\, ^{0.15}_{0.17} \, \text{(stat)} \pm 0.07 \, \text{(syst)}, \\
  \CDD &=  1.73 \pm\, ^{0.16}_{0.17} \, \text{(stat)} \pm 0.024 \, \text{(syst)}.
\end{align*}
\end{abstract}
